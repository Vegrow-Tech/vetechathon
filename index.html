Introduction_

Welcome to VeTechathon!

Get ready to embark on an exhilarating journey of innovation and creativity! This first ever Vegrow hackathon is not just about solving problems; it's about dreaming big, pushing boundaries, and transforming groundbreaking ideas into reality. Over the course of two days, we're unleashing the power of innovation to drive change and create meaningful impact.

Why Participate ?

INNOVATION


This hackathon is your chance to think outside the box, challenge conventions, and pioneer new solutions.

COLLABORATION


Connect with like-minded colleagues, collaborate across teams, and leverage diverse perspectives to fuel your projects.

FREEDOM


No constraints! Work on any idea that excites you, whether it's solving a critical business problem or exploring a passion project.

FUN

While we're serious about innovation, we also believe in fostering a vibrant and enjoyable atmosphere. Expect energizing activities, team bonding moments, and plenty of snacks to keep you fueled.

Evaluation Criteria_

Criteria Description Weightage
Impact Potential impact on improving current processes or operations. Ability to address specific pain points or challenges within the industry. Potential to open new business opportunities or revenue streams. 20%
Innovation Uniqueness and creativity of the solution. Novelty in addressing challenges or improving existing processes. Use of cutting-edge technologies or approaches. 15%
Technical Excellence Quality of code and architecture. Efficient use of technology stack and tools. Consideration of best practices and coding standards. 15%
Feasibility and Adaptability Viability and feasibility of the MVP in real-world scenarios. Scalability of the solution to accommodate future growth. Consideration of technical, operational, and financial feasibility. Flexibility and adaptability of the solution to evolving requirements. Potential for future iterations or enhancements based on feedback. 15%
User Experience (UX) and Design Intuitiveness and ease of use for end-users. Improvement in user satisfaction and engagement. Consideration of accessibility and inclusivity principles. 10%
Functionality Completeness and effectiveness in addressing the defined problem statement. Core features and functionalities implemented. Reliability and robustness of the solution. 10%
Presentation Clarity and effectiveness of the pitch or presentation. Ability to communicate the problem statement, solution, and value proposition. Engagement with the audience and ability to address questions effectively. 5%
Collaboration and Team Dynamics Team synergy, effective communication, and balanced contribution from all members. 5%

FAQs_

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 4.

Can I come individually?

No. A team of minimum 2 members is allowed.

When?

HackRcdu 2020 will be a 18 hours hackathon which will stretch from 27th February to 28th February 2020 (without the overnight stay)

Where?

HackRcdu will be conducted on the college campus of Ramanujan College, Delhi University - Kalkaji.

Will there be food arrangement?

Oh-Yeah-Absolutely. You will be provided with proper meals on both days. Thanks to our sponsors.

How much does it cost?

Everything at Hackrcdu is free of cost.

What are the criteria of shortlisting the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted based on their GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your school/college ID, laptops, phone, chargers, extensions.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student from any University/College or Higher Secondary School in India.
